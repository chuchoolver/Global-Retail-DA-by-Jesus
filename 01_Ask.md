# Ask

## 1. Project Overview

### Project Name

Global Retail Analytics Dashboard

### Business Domain

Retail Analytics

### Project Description

This project analyzes historical retail sales data to evaluate business performance, identify sales trends, and provide actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The project follows the Google Data Analytics framework: Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Share, and Act.

---

## 2. Business Problem

The company generates thousands of sales transactions across different markets, product categories, and customer segments. However, decision-makers lack a centralized and interactive solution to monitor business performance, identify profitable areas, detect underperforming products, and evaluate customer and regional trends.

Without clear visibility into these metrics, it becomes difficult to make informed business decisions and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## 3. Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Monitor overall sales and profitability performance.
- Identify the best and worst performing products and categories.
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Evaluate regional and market performance.
- Understand customer segment behavior.
- Measure the impact of discounts on profit.
- Provide interactive reports that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 4. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Business Need |
|--------------|---------------|
| Executive Management | Monitor overall business performance and KPIs. |
| Sales Manager | Track sales performance across products, categories, and regions. |
| Marketing Manager | Understand customer segments and purchasing behavior. |
| Operations Manager | Analyze shipping methods and operational efficiency. |
| Business Analysts | Explore data and identify business opportunities. |

---

## 5. Business Questions

This dashboard aims to answer the following business questions:

1. How are sales, profit, and quantity evolving over time?
2. Which markets and regions generate the highest revenue?
3. Which product categories and subcategories are the most profitable?
4. Which products generate the highest sales?
5. Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
6. How do discounts affect profitability?
7. Which shipping methods are used most frequently?
8. Which regions require additional business attention?
9. What are the key performance indicators of the business?
10. What insights can help improve business performance?

---

## 6. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it delivers:

- A clean and interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Executive KPIs in a single view.
- Interactive filtering by year, market, region, category, and customer segment.
- Meaningful visualizations that answer all business questions.
- Actionable insights supported by data.
- A professional documentation following the Google Data Analytics process.
