# Ask

## Business Problem

The company generates thousands of sales transactions across different markets, product categories, and customer segments. However, decision-makers lack a centralized and interactive solution to monitor business performance, identify profitable areas, detect underperforming products, and evaluate customer and regional trends.

Without clear visibility into these metrics, it becomes difficult to make informed business decisions and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales and profitability performance.
- Identify the best and worst performing products and categories.
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Evaluate regional and market performance.
- Understand customer segment behavior.
- Measure the impact of discounts on profit.
- Support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Business Need |
|--------------|---------------|
| Executive Management | Monitor business performance and KPIs. |
| Sales Manager | Analyze sales performance. |
| Marketing Manager | Understand customer behavior. |
| Operations Manager | Evaluate shipping performance. |
| Business Analysts | Identify business opportunities. |

---

## Business Questions

1. How are sales and profit evolving over time?
2. Which markets generate the highest revenue?
3. Which product categories are the most profitable?
4. Which products generate the highest sales?
5. Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
6. How do discounts affect profitability?
7. Which shipping methods are most frequently used?
8. Which regions require additional attention?
9. Which KPIs best represent business performance?
10. What insights can support better business decisions?

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- The dashboard answers all business questions.
- Users can interactively explore the data.
- KPIs are clearly presented.
- Business insights are supported by data.
- Actionable recommendations are provided.
